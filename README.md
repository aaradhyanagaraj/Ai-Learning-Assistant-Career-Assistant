#  AI Learning Assistant & Career Assistant

This project is a simple AI-powered assistant designed to help students and job seekers prepare for their careers.  
It combines learning support, interview practice, and cover letter generation in one application.

The app is built using **Python and Streamlit**, and it uses **LLMs through Ollama** to generate responses.  
Users can also upload documents like resumes, notes, or job descriptions to get more personalized assistance.

---

##  What This Project Can Do

###  AI Learning Assistant
You can ask questions just like chatting with an AI tutor.  
You can also upload documents and ask questions related to those files.

Supported files include:
- PDF
- DOCX
- TXT
- Images (text extracted using OCR)

---

###  AI Interview Simulator
This feature helps you practice interviews.

How it works:
1. Upload your resume
2. Enter the job role you are applying for
3. The AI asks interview questions
4. You answer them
5. The AI evaluates your answers and gives feedback

It also provides:
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Suggestions
- A final interview score

---

###  AI Cover Letter Generator
This feature helps you quickly generate a professional cover letter.

You can upload:
- Your resume
- A job description

The AI will generate a personalized cover letter based on both.

---

###  Chat Features
The application also includes some helpful chat tools:

- Start new chats
- View chat history
- Export conversations
- Share chat text
